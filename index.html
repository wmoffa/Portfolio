My Bio

Originally from the Bronx New York, I now live in North Brunswick New Jersey. I grew up in the neighborhood that was near Yankee Stadium, so on many a summers day you know where you would find me


For a long time now, I have been paid as a software developer. Notice that I did not say 'I worked' as software developer. The reason is that I enjoyed every minute of it .. and I still have that compassion today!


I have said this to many young people who are deciding on a career: choose something that you love to do and you will never 'work' for living. I can not imagine being forced to do something that I did not enjoy.


Now I am not saying it was all easy - in fact, at times, it was very difficult - but I love to be challenged to figure out a solution to a problem.


This career has given me the opportunity to travel around the world and have had the previlage to work with many talented and creative people along the way.
